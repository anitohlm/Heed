# Heed — Market Research

This document presents the market opportunity for Heed, with sourced data where available and explicit founder-estimate labels where data is not.

---

## 1. Market Size

### Philippines productivity app market

- **Smartphone penetration:** 64M smartphone users in the Philippines as of 2024 (Statista, 2024).
- **Mobile-first behavior:** the Philippines ranks among the highest globally in time spent on mobile apps daily (DataReportal, 2024).
- **Productivity software market (PH):** approximately USD 280M in 2024, projected CAGR 12-14% through 2028 (IDC, 2024).
- **Wellness and self-care app spend (PH):** rising sharply post-pandemic; Cognitive Market Research (2023) estimates PH digital wellness market at USD 95M, projected to double by 2028.

### Mental health and executive function context

- **Mental health prevalence:** PSA NICTHS 2024 reports approximately 14.5% of Filipinos aged 15+ self-report symptoms consistent with anxiety or depressive disorders.
- **ADHD diagnosis trends:** Fortune Business Insights (2024) shows global adult ADHD diagnoses up 42% since 2020. PH-specific data is limited but follows the global trend qualitatively.
- **Founder estimate:** the addressable PH segment of "adults with high mental load needing assistance with recurring task management" is approximately 4-6M people. This estimate is derived from the smartphone-using working-age population intersected with self-reported productivity-app interest.

### Regional expansion targets

- **Indonesia:** 196M smartphone users, similar mental-load profile, comparable smartphone-first economy (Statista, 2024).
- **Vietnam:** 78M smartphone users, fastest-growing productivity app downloads in SEA (App Annie, 2024).

**Heed's positioning gap:** no existing tool combines (1) agentic cadence learning grounded in actual user behavior, (2) Filipino cultural context (Taglish, GCash, PH calendar), and (3) trust-first product design that avoids the nagging/shaming pattern common in habit apps.

---

## 3. Why Now

Three converging factors make Heed buildable and valuable in 2026:

1. **Agentic AI matured in 2024-2025.** Tool-use reliability, multi-step planning, and autonomous background updates became production-grade. A product like Heed was not technically feasible two years ago.

2. **PH mobile economy reached scale.** GCash penetration crossed 80% of adult smartphone users in 2024 (BSP, 2024). Local payment infrastructure for subscription products is now reliable.

3. **Mental health awareness in PH increased post-pandemic.** Productivity-app users are more willing to identify as "having executive function struggles" than they were pre-2020. The neurodivergent product positioning is no longer a barrier.

## 5. What We Still Don't Know

Acknowledged unknowns at hackathon submission time:

- **Conversion rate from free to paid.** Founder estimate of 3-5% is derived from comparable PH consumer SaaS but unvalidated for this specific category.
- **Retention curve.** No data yet on whether agentic cadence learning produces stronger retention than rule-based task apps.
- **Willingness to share data with the agent.** Some users may resist the "agent that watches my patterns" positioning; the actual rate is unknown.
- **GCash recurring billing UX.** The technical integration path is known; the user-experience friction at the conversion point is not yet measured.

These will be answered during the post-hackathon beta phase.
